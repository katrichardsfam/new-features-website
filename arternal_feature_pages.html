INTAKE AGENT — HOW IT WORKS

Four steps. Zero manual data entry.

The Intake Agent turns any document into a structured artwork record, then hands it to you for a final check.

1
Upload your document
Upload a PDF, text message, artist submission, or pull artwork data straight from Smart Mail.
2
AI extracts every field
Title, artist, medium, dimensions, edition info, year. Pulled from the document with source attribution for each field.
3
Review and edit
See every extracted value alongside its source. Edit anything that needs correcting. Duplicate detection flags existing records automatically.
4
Approve and create
One click to confirm. The artwork record is created in your inventory with images included. Nothing is saved until you say so.
